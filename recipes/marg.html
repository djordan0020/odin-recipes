The Margarita

Margaritas are a mixed drink with bold flavors made with tequila.

A margarita is a drink that is incredibly easy to make and just as delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces blanco tequila
  • 1/2 ounce orange liqueur
  • 1 ounce lime juice
  • 1/2 ounce agave syrup
  • Lime slice and salt for garnish

Steps

  1. Mix liquids in shaker with ice.
  2. Strain into glass over ice.
  3. Salt rim and garnish with lime slice.
