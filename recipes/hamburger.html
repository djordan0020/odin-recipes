Hamburger

Hamburgers are a classic American food popular at a variety of social events, restaurants, home dining rooms, and backyard barbeque's.

This savory sandwhich is simple in preparation, cooking, and assembly, but offers multiple flavor profiles. Below are the ingredients and steps to prepare this dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 6 hamburger buns

Optional Ingridients

  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Mushrooms

Steps

  1. 1. Form ground beef into 6 patties.
  2. 2. Heat pan to medium heat.
  3. 3. Flip patty when half cooked.
  4. 4. Add patty and optional toppings to bun.
